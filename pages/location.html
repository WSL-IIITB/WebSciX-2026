Venue & Travel

IIIT Bangalore, Electronics City — November 26–28, 2026

Bengaluru, Karnataka

Silicon Valley of India — a city of innovation, heritage, and hospitality

Bengaluru is widely recognised as India's technology capital, home to a dense ecosystem of multinational companies, high-growth startups, and premier research institutions including IISc, ISRO, and DRDO. The city has driven India's leadership in software exports, deep technology, and AI research, attracting sustained global investment.

As a destination for international academic conferences, Bengaluru offers excellent connectivity, a range of accommodation options, and a cosmopolitan, welcoming environment. Its traditions in classical music, art, literature, and cuisine coexist with its modern identity — creating an inspiring backdrop for global scholarly exchange.

Conference Venue

IIIT Bangalore

Address: 26/C, Electronics City Phase 1, Hosur Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560100, India

The International Institute of Information Technology Bangalore (IIIT-B) is a premier university focusing on Information Technology and its applications. The campus is located in Electronics City, Bengaluru's primary technology hub.

Main Conference Hall

Plenary sessions and keynotes will be held in the main auditorium on campus.

Workshop Rooms

Parallel workshop and tutorial sessions will be held in seminar rooms.

Accommodation

Several hotels are located within 2–5 km of the IIIT Bangalore campus in the Electronics City area. A list of recommended hotels with negotiated conference rates will be published closer to the conference. We encourage participants to book early as November is a busy season for Bengaluru.

For participants preferring central Bengaluru, areas such as Koramangala, HSR Layout, and Silk Board offer good connectivity to Electronics City via road, namma metro and bus.

Visa Information

International participants requiring an Indian visa are advised to apply well in advance. India offers an e-Visa facility for citizens of many countries, available through the official Indian e-Visa portal. The conference organisation can provide official invitation letters upon request to support visa applications.

Please contact the Organising Chair (Dr. Sushree S. Behera) for invitation letters: sushree.behera@iiitb.ac.in
