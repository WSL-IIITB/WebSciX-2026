📄 Call for Papers

WebSciX 2026

Theme: 20 Years of Web Science: Navigating the Era of Generative and Agentic AI

The Asia-Pacific Satellite Event of ACM WebSci

📍 Bengaluru, India | 📅 November 26–28, 2026

Invitation to Submit

WebSciX 2026 invites researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to submit their original work for its inaugural conference, poised as a regional satellite event of the global ACM WebSci conference.

WebSciX 2026 is dedicated to advancing the interdisciplinary study of the Internet, World Wide Web (WWW), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) and their impact on human lives in the Asia-Pacific region. The conference aims to build an accessible regional community of experts to investigate the socio-technical dynamics of digital ecosystems within the unique socio-cultural, economic, and technological contexts of the Asia-Pac regions.

As we celebrate two decades of Web Science, the digital landscape is undergoing a seismic shift. The Web is no longer merely a repository of information or a medium for human-to-human interaction; it has become a substrate for autonomous entities. The rise of Agentic AI—systems capable of reasoning, planning, and executing multi-step goals with minimal human oversight—presents profound challenges and opportunities for humanity.

🛡️ Academic Integrity Policies

Plagiarism

Plagiarism, in any form, is strictly prohibited. All sources must be properly cited. Any submission found to be plagiarized will be rejected.

AI-Generated Content

Submissions primarily authored by generative AI models are not acceptable. AI tools may be used as assistants, but core intellectual contribution must be human-authored. Disclose significant AI usage in submissions.
